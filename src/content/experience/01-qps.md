Full stack development in an agile team across Queensland Police's digital estate.

- Delivered the new **Marine Rescue Queensland** website (mrq.qld.gov.au) end to end
  from Figma designs — custom modules, custom theme, Paragraphs-based layout
  architecture, Views and taxonomy structures.
- Ongoing platform support and feature development for **police.qld.gov.au**,
  **fire.qld.gov.au** and **protectiveservicesgroup.qld.gov.au**.
- Optimised frontend performance with React components integrated with Drupal via
  **GraphQL**; designed custom GraphQL APIs secured with **OAuth2**.
- Reduced page load time by **40%** through AWS S3 asset optimisation.
- Established regression testing framework with PHPUnit, Cypress and Selenium.
- Replaced unsupported contrib modules, built and maintained complex webforms
  (including .NET-backed form pages), ensured AA/AAA compliance across all pages,
  and worked with the DevOps team on per-environment pipeline rules.
